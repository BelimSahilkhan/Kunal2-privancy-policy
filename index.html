Privacy Policy

My Dairy

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Customer or farmer details entered by users
  • Daily collection records and quantity data
  • Payment details (if entered)
  • Device information for performance and security

2. Google Sign-In

My Dairy allows users to sign in using their Google account. We may receive your name, email address, and profile photo for account identification. This information is used only to enable login functionality.

3. Local Data Storage

Records and customer data are stored locally on your device using secure local database storage. This allows offline access and full control over your data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Manage daily records efficiently
  • Track customer and quantity data
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Provide better record management

5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data. Your records remain private and are not shared without your permission.

6. Data Sharing Policy

We do NOT sell, share, or distribute your data. All records remain under your control.

7. Permissions Used

  • Internet access - for Google Sign-In
  • Storage access - to save and retrieve records

8. User Control

  • You can edit or delete records anytime
  • You can sign out of your account
  • You can uninstall the app to remove local data

9. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for business and record management use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
